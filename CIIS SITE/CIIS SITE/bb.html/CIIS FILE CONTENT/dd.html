ABOUT CIIS

Think Innovate Rise

The Continental Institute made its forays into field of education in 2000. In such a short span, it has carved a niche for itself in the field of imparting quality education of International standard. Since its inception, the Institute has progressed leaps and bounds and has established Premier Institute in the field of Engineering, Computer Science & Application, IT, Commerce and Management at both graduate and under graduate levels. Continental Institute has brought various programs under one roof thereby facilitating the students to pursue education of their passion. It is our conviction that personal and intimate attention inspires the students to develop self-confidence, professional knowledge and contemporary skill
