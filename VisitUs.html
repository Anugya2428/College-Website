About CIIS

Continental Institute for International Studies made its forays into field of education in 2000. In such a short span, it has carved a niche for itself in the field of imparting quality education of International standard. Since its inception, the Institute has progressed leaps and bounds and has established Premier Institute in the field of Hospitality, Engineering, Information Technology, Commerce and Management at both graduate and under graduate levels.

Students can enroll in and finish their first two semesters of a Canadian diploma or degree program through the International Pathway Programs offered by CIIS and can then finish their course in Canada with a 100% credit transfer of two semesters / one year.
